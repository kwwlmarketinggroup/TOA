Our Story

We would like you to be part of the family.

The Taste of Africa is a family-owned business whose goal is to provide a warm family ambience and serve a variety of healthy, tasty Central African cuisine.

The menu features specialties from Congo, Gabon, and Kenya, with dine-in, carry-out, banquet, and catering services available for the community.

“My family originated from the Republic of Congo. In the early 1990s a civil war broke out and my family was forced to run and leave everything behind.”

After arriving in Upstate New York in August of 2005, it became a dream to open a restaurant and share a love of African cuisine with the local community. After years of planning and perseverance, that dream became The Taste of Africa in October of 2017.

We are here to serve you and accommodate your needs. We welcome you to enjoy the delicious tastes of Africa.
